## What Makes NHLPLAY Special

### Live Game Experience
Follow games as they happen with real-time updates every 10 seconds. See scoring plays, shot counts, penalties, and momentum shifts without refreshing. When the game ends, get instant access to full boxscores, three stars, and official NHL recap videos.

### Pre-Game Intelligence
Before puck drop, get the edge with detailed matchup analysis. Our Game Advantage Calculator crunches the numbers on team statistics, recent performance, and head-to-head history to show which team has the statistical advantage.

### Player Profiles That Go Deep
Click any player to open their full profile with season stats, career numbers, and advanced analytics. See how they compare to league average with radar charts, check their last 5 games, and explore NHL EDGE tracking data like top speed and distance skated.

### Track Your Favorite Players
Log in and add players to your personal tracker. Get a quick dashboard view of how all your tracked players are performing this season, complete with points, goals, and recent game highlights.

### Team Builder & Draft Mode
Build your dream lineup with our drag-and-drop team builder. Challenge yourself with Draft Mode, picking players round by round.

## Feedback & Suggestions

This repository is for collecting feedback, bug reports, and feature requests from NHLPLAY users.

### How to Submit Feedback

1. **Bug Reports**: Open an issue with the `bug` label. Include what happened, what you expected, and your browser/device.

2. **Feature Requests**: Open an issue with the `enhancement` label. Describe the feature and why it would be useful.

3. **General Feedback**: Open an issue with the `feedback` label. We read everything!

### Before Submitting

- Search existing issues to avoid duplicates
- Be as specific as possible
- Screenshots help a lot for visual issues
